In the forgotten corners of twilight Paris, where candlelight trembles on rain-slick streets,
a secret laboratory once held the impossible — cats forged from science and starlight.
Under the cold gaze of Dr. Viktor Grave, they lived as experiments, not souls.

Then came Yeshua — quiet, steadfast, and brave — whose compassion broke their chains.
Through storm and shadow, he led them to freedom, to a refuge of warmth and wonder.
There, amidst coffee steam and moonlit glass, he opened the Chimera Cats Café —
a sanctuary for dreamers, where magic purrs softly between every heartbeat.

But legends do not rest easily. The scientists still whisper in the dark,
seeking the power they once commanded. And so the Café endures,
half myth, half miracle — a beacon for all who have ever been caged.

Dedicated to the dreamers, the wanderers, and every soul who finds beauty in the impossible.
May your spirit remain untamed.
